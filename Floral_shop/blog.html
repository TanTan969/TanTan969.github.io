Choosing The Right Flowers For Different Occasions





Flowers make a great gift regardless of the occasion. However, it is pertinent to realize that each flower is best for a specific situation. For instance, you probably wouldn’t want to give someone a bunch of roses for a funeral. Within this comprehensive guide, you will learn about the best flowers for each unique scenario. This will help to ensure that your flowers are well received and thoroughly appreciated.

Valentine’s Day

Valentine’s Day is a time to show your love for your significant other. It is all about romance and you should use the occasion to strengthen your relationship with your partner. For the upcoming Valentine’s Day, you should definitely give roses. Roses reek of romance. Most colors work exceptionally well for this holiday, but you should steer clear of yellow since it represents friendship. If you’re not a fan of roses, you can switch to tulips. Both will work great.
Just remember to always take your significant other’s preferences into account when attempting to choose flowers for Valentine’s Day!

Anniversary

Your anniversary is a time to remember. It is a holiday to celebrate your relationship with the love of your life. So of course, you’ll want to celebrate it with a bundle of flowers. The good news is that most flowers will work well for this unique scenario. Going with seasonal flowers is a good idea and much more convenient. Alternatively, you want to consider roses or lilies. These flowers can help to send a message of love and a bright future. Just be sure to stay away from carnations, since they usually equate to disappointment. Again, it is pertinent to take your significant other’s preferences and wishes into consideration when attempting to choose flowers for this event.

Funerals

A funeral is a sad affair, they give loved ones a time to grieve the loss of another. To ensure that you’re able to brighten the mood slightly, you should consider giving the individual a condolence flower arrangement. Arrangements work far better for traditional funerals than bouquets. Your color options are plentiful. You can choose between lilies, chrysanthemum or roses. Generally, the former two will work best. Simultaneously, the best colors for a funeral would include blue, white and green. Remember to avoid bright colors.

Birthdays

You’ll definitely want to celebrate the occasion with your friends or loved ones. The good thing about Birthdays is that you should feel free to use your creativity. In general, flowers are the best for female friends. Nevertheless, it is not rare for men to receive flowers. When it comes to birthdays, your options are endless. You can pretty much choose anything and still hit a home-run. For instance, you could give the receiver the flower of the month. Alternatively, you should stick with roses, lilies, tulips, gerberas or anything in between.
Try to guess the individual’s favorite color for maximum effect!

Mother’s Day

Mother’s Day is definitely one of the most important times of the year. During this day, it is time to give back to the woman, who raised you and taught you to be the special individual that you are today. With this in mind, you will want to go all out to ensure that your mother has an amazing Mother’s Day! Flowers work great because they symbolize beauty and nature. To make your mother happy, you should consider giving her gerberas, lilies or carnations. The best colors are generally white, purple, pink or red.
These flower options will work great because they’ll prove that you’re thankful and appreciative of your mother’s love and kindness.

Wedding

The wedding is one of the most amazing events in anyone’s life. It allows two individuals to officially interlink their lives together and to celebrate their love. The wedding is all about reflection and romance. So, you’ll definitely want to try and symbolize both with your flower choices. When attempting to choose flowers for the upcoming wedding, you should try to consider the preferences of the bride and groom. This aspect can make it a little trickier. Or, you may want to consider making the switch to seasonal flowers.
When it comes to colors, you should do your best to stick with the wedding’s color scheme. Find out what type of theme the couple has selected and go from there. The most common color choices will be pink, purple, and red. Roses and Hydrangeas always work for weddings, since they ooze romance and love!

Get Well Wishes

People believe when one of their friends, family members or close acquaintances gets ill it will make them feel better if they give them flowers. However, this is only a myth that has never debunked. Giving someone flower when they are sick shows respect and you care for them. So, the next time someone you know gets sick, be sure to give him or her flowers. Now, the type of flowers you give them is another question.
When people are in the hospital, they do not need to be given flowers that emit a strong odor. Instead, stick with florist flowers that offer little to no scent. You should also limit the number of flowers you give to three or four and toss in a teddy bear or balloon into the mixture, just to add a little fun.

Congratulatory Events

There is a good chance that everyone will be congratulated at some point or another. Almost everyone is going to graduate high school, performance or have a baby. Each of these events calls for a celebration and a congratulation. When congratulations are in order, it is pertinent to make sure you opt for the right flowers. The truth of the matter is that each event calls for different flowers. For instance, graduation would be more suitable for gerberas and sunflowers, whereas a pregnancy might be best for lilies.
Generally, congratulatory events will call for bright, bold colors. Also, try to opt for happy flowers, such as sunflowers, tulips, or orchids. This combination will show that the receiver is going to have a bright and happy future!
